## 📊 Data Insights — Stats Tab

This is where Mochi Mode goes beyond a regular productivity app.
The Stats tab is a **mini data science dashboard** built from 
your real Pomodoro session data.

### 🔢 Key Metrics
Metric : What It Means <br>
**Consistency %** : Measures how evenly you study each day using **Coefficient of Variation (CV = σ/μ)**. 100% = no cramming! <br>
**Total Focus** : Sum of all your Pomodoro minutes  <br>
**Peak Hour** : The clock hour when you start the most sessions  <br>
**Std Dev / Day** : Standard deviation of daily focus minutes — lower = more consistent <br>

### 📅 Focus Heatmap
- Inspired by GitHub's contribution graph
- Each square = one day, darker pink = more focus time
- Shows last **14 weeks** of activity
- Hover any square to see exact date and minutes
- Gaps (pale squares) = days you didn't study — aim to fill them!

### 📈 Focus Flow — 30 Days
- Line chart of daily focus minutes over 30 days
- **Pink area** = raw daily minutes
- **Dashed purple line** = 7-day moving average (MA) to show your true trend
- Shows **Mean/Day**, **Std Dev**, and **total Sessions**
- Rising MA line = your study habit is improving!

### ⚡ This Week
- Bar chart of focus minutes per day (last 7 days)
- Today's bar highlighted in deep pink
- Helps identify which days you're most productive
- Useful for planning when to schedule hard study sessions

### 💡 Your Study Summary
- Auto-generated insight based on your data
- Shows total deep focus hours + consistency score
- Calculated using **coefficient of variation** (lower daily variance = higher score)
